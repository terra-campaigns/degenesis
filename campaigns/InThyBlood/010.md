## Hours of destiny

The group sees [Lucatore](../../directory/Purgare/Lucatore/index.md) at dusk light in the valley.
Two processions with a few hundred people each follow the main road, from opposite sides.
They carry villagers' tools and torches.

[Thread](../../directory/Purgare/AltairEnvoys/Thread.md) avoids it by walking alongside the wall of the village.
[Emissary Neva](../../directory/Purgare/TheCloister/Neva.md) goes with the young Chronicler, and invites him to the [Cloister](../../directory/Purgare/TheCloister/index.md).

> "You are still young, who knows, there might be a good Anabaptist inside you. I've made worse bets in the past."

Thread declines for now, he has one single thing in mind: find [Decoy 5](../../directory/Purgare/Lucatore/Decoy5.md).
At the rendezvous point, the sign: the battery has been collected - Decoy 5 will be looking for Thread.
Content with the finding, Thread climbs the track that leads to the Cloister.
A butterfly lands on his hand.
The Purgan chakra can be seen on its blue and yellow wings.

[Maren](../../directory/Purgare/AltairEnvoys/Maren.md) and [Nero Vex](../../directory/Purgare/AltairEnvoys/NeroVex.md) proceed to the village.
Maren reckons she needs medicine to fight her spore infestation.
They avoid facing the processions, and circle it towards the [Bleeding Ram](../../directory/Purgare/Lucatore/BleedingRam.md), the Spitalian said he is stay somewhere close.

## The last sign

> "Are you out of your minds? Folks, these Flayers are under arrest!"

Shouts the Anabaptist procession, coming from the east.
Sitting on a throne of wood, with a boy in his lap, [Scirocco](../../directory/Purgare/Lucatore/Scirocco.md) preaches.
Behind him, a group of flayers bring a half naked, bound woman: [Dana](../../directory/Purgare/Lucatore/Dana.md).

> "Look here, brothers and sisters! This wretched woman is possessed by the Demiurge. She has suckled her baby with blood instead of divine milk!"

They start flaying and hurting the woman.
The Anabaptist procession pleads for them to stop, she needs only be baptised with water.

![](../../imgs/gallery/Pasted%20image%2020260516091044.png)

> "Here, bring the barrel."

The Flayers continue to hurt Dana.

Maren finds her way to climb a house by the Flayer's procession while Nero lay a rope in front of the unaware mob and enters, finding himself a torch.
The young Apocalyptic shouts and sets fire to a hay stack and a crate of wood.
The mob becomes uncomfortable and than BANG!
Scirocco's head explodes with Maren's sniper shot.

The procession turns into a disorganised evacuation.
People stumble on the rope.
Fire spreads.
In the confusion, Dana flees towards the Anabaptists.

## Madness

The Anabaptists receive Dana with open arms.

> "Only the Baptism purifies the sinful body, washes out the worm, cleanses the blood. We, the Anabaptists, rule the water. Ours is the Baptism!"

Once they open the barrel of water there is pure horror.
The barrel is filled with tapeworms.
Dana, scared, kicks it towards the crowd: it falls, breaks and splashes.
Another procession becomes a tumult.

Nero and Maren take Dana to safety, but another woman cries in the mob.
The remaining flayers now have [Verena](../../directory/Purgare/TheCloister/Verena.md).
One holds a ceremonial stone dagger.

> "Almighty God! Ruler over"

BANG! Maren shoots and cut off the flayer's hand.

> "This is God flaying your body brother! Carry on! He wants it!"

> "In Thy name we will rise above the..."

BANG! A second shot goes through the man's torso.
Maren shouts at them to stop, otherwise she will kill them all.

A deafening buzzing sound.
It grows darker, the moon is covered.
A swarm descends in Lucatore.

> "IN THY BLOOD we will wash the sins from our souls and consecrate the innocent!"

The insects start to eat everyone alive.
Maren knows she can save herself or Verena.
She apologises to the girl and catches up with Nero and Dana, who run and enter the Ambrio's chapel while being bitten.

![](../../imgs/gallery/Pasted%20image%2020260516111347.png)

## A mirror of hell

From the top of the trail that leads to the Cloister Thread can see the swarm descending.
The bugs are sparse enough here to not offer a big danger.
From afar he sees Lucatore burning.
To the east a reflection of that, as if it is a mirror.

Continuing the climb he now sees half a body, with its "reflection" half a meter away.
The cause of death is clear, but what could have cut it in half like this?
Thread has bigger plans, and carry on to the Cloister, where he is received.
Before going to sleep in the chapel, the Chronicler notices the entrance to the tower is a small door behind Neva's desk, on her chamber.

Before entering Ambrio's chapel Marren still sees ants carrying Sirroco's half eaten corpse.
The body is black, covered in maggots and insects.
The ground opens, a dark hole swallows the flayer.
