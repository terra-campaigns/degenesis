## The swarm moves

The swarm starts to move out of the village towards the [Cloister](../../directory/Purgare/TheCloister/index.md).
[Maren](../../directory/Purgare/AltairEnvoys/Maren.md) organises a fire brigade to put out the fires in [Lucatore](../../directory/Purgare/Lucatore/index.md).
[Nero Vex](../../directory/Purgare/AltairEnvoys/NeroVex.md) goes out through the gates and finds the chapel master consumed by the primer inside the Visconti chapel.
The corpse does not show the stigmata on his chest, as one would expect.

In the Cloister, [Anastasia](../../directory/Pollen/ProtectorateClique/Anastasia.md) hears the shouts of order from Abbacus:

> "To your weapons! Ready the Spitfires! The swarm is coming!

[Thread](../../directory/Purgare/AltairEnvoys/Thread.md) moves towards [Neva](../../directory/Purgare/TheCloister/Neva.md)'s room again, and sit still to listen.
From the tower, behind her chambers, a flapping of a thousand butterflies.
They leave the tower in every direction.
The Spitfires start, flames light up the night, killing the swarm.

Maren finds [Verena](../../directory/Purgare/TheCloister/Verena.md) again in a corner of the village.
The girl is scared but not hurt.
As the Hellvetic tries to move the girl to a safe location, the first butterflies appear.

> "Let me go, Lady, I need to help my friend [Vikal](../../directory/Purgare/TheCloister/Vikal.md)!"

She points at the amber amulet she carries on her neck.

## Awakening

A sudden, gut-wrenching blast of sound consumes everyone.
Bodies collapse and, for minutes, Lucatore is almost at a standstill.
No one but Anastasia [has heard this before ](../ConnectNikopol/InNikopol01.md).

In the village, Maren finds Nero.
Both run up towards the Cloister, accompanying Verena.

From where Anastasia sees, the tower bulges after the sound.
And in a second, it explodes, throwing bricks everywhere.
By now some Anabaptists had fallen eaten by insects or with their ears burst.
A few more suffer as bricks fly their way.

> "No Vikal!, noo!

The air continues to thicken, the heat continues to grow.
It is what Thread listens to, and a minute later, the body of Neva is thrown at the other side of the door.
With Anastasia, they enter the room and see Vikal, enraged, red-eyed, looking at the body of his mother.
He speaks with a voice that is not a child's.

> "He is coming."

High-pressure steam starts to escape from fissures in the ground.
The oil mills downstairs are overheating.

## Deadly terror

On the trail, outside the cloister, Maren sees the [large Psychonaut](../../directory/Purgare/Lucatore/Barghest.md) climbing the rock towards the walls.
She shoots the creature's hand, which now hangs in one hand, and it shoots back a lance of light from its eyes.
Maren dodges in the last minute, and only the left side of her armour is damaged.
When aiming for a second shot, the Psychonaut is gone.

After Maren's shot, Vikal falls unconscious.
The swarm also seems to have weakened.

A few minutes later, the group regathers.
No one knows how to use the amber amulet to help Vikal.
While Anastasia researches, the others guard the windows and door.

Amidst the mist of hot steam, the light of Spitfires, and the darkness of the swarm, they see the Psychonaut again.
It is now within the cloister.
The Anabaptists turn their aim at it.

> **"WOMAN HAS BARGHEST’S SON!“**

No one expected the titan to speak.

## Seed and fire

Anastasia figures it out.
The amber holds a seed of a [Fractal Forest](../ConnectNikopol/ToNikopol2.md).
How would this save Vikal?
No one knows.
Anastasia makes him swallow it.

Outside, the Phychonaut named Barghest towers in front of the Anabaptists.
Maren continues to shoot at it.
No shot is missed, but the creature does not stop.
From a corner, Nero brings a fuel tank from one of the Spitfires and damages it until it leaks.
He places it atop one of the steam fissures.

Barghest continues to advance.
The heat continues to go up.
Vikal wakes up as a child again.

> "Mama!"

He speaks for the first time, but also the last.
Soon, he starts coughing up mucus and blood.
The seed cured him, but also killed him.

BANG!

The fuel tank explodes.
The ground collapses, taking Barghest with it.
When the creature hits the lower level, its sheer weight collapses it too.
Fire rains into the oil mills, under the cloister.
It explodes, and an incendiary fire takes over the cloister.

Barghest is dead.

But also any hope of continuing [Altair](../../directory/Purgare/HouseBenesato/Altair.md)'s Elysian Oil production.
