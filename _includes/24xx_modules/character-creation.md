# Character Creation

Chroniclers maintain a record of all known subjects.
Records can be extracted and printed on their handheld devices.

Use their output to play a young PC.

## Life-path

Subject records might be out of date when chroniclers retrieve them.
Once the subject data update retrieval is complete, they are ready to participate in memetic simulations.

Use the protocol below and roll skills and potentials on the **CONCEPT**, **CULTURE**, and **CULT** tables.
