Records can be extracted and printed on their handheld devices.

## Updating Personnel Data

A subject's data may be updated with the Chronicler protocol `NODE-SYNC 2.4A`.

```
---------------------------------------------------------------------------
>>   S Y S T E M   N O T I C E   —   D O T - M A T R I X   O U T P U T   <<
---------------------------------------------------------------------------

I N I T I A T I N G    P R O T O C O L :   N O D E - S Y N C   2 . 4 A

: 1 CONSULT CLASS D6 .............................................

  :1.1 CONSULT TWICE AGE BAND HANDICAP ........................
       EXPELLED FROM CULT.... UPDATE CULT TO CLANNER, FIRST RANK
       *** NO FURTHER UPDATES ENCOUNTERED *** .........[END]......

  :1.2 CONSULT ONCE AGE BAND HANDICAP ..........................
       *** NO FURTHER UPDATES ENCOUNTERED *** ........[END]......

  :1.3 CONSULT ONCE AGE BAND HANDICAP .............................
       UPDATE APTITUDE PROFILE, CONSULT CULTURAL DATABASES.......
       *** FURTHER UPDATES MAY EXIST ***.................[OK]..

  :1.4 CONSULT ONCE AGE BAND HANDICAP .............................
       UPDATE APTITUDE PROFILE, CONSULT CULT DATABASES.......
       UPDATE POTENTIAL, CONSULT CULT DATABASES...............
       *** FURTHER UPDATES MAY EXIST ***...............[░░░OK░░]..

  :1.5 CONSULT ONCE AGE BAND HANDICAP .............................
       RANK PROMOTION .... SUBJECT MEETS REQUIREMENTS.............
       *** FURTHER UPDATES MAY EXIST ***.................[OK]..

  :1.6 RANK PROMOTION .... SUBJECT MEETS REQUIREMENTS.............
       UPDATE APTITUDE PROFILE, CONSULT SUBJECT TYPE DATABASES.....
       *** FURTHER UPDATES MAY EXIST ***..................[OK]..

: 2 UPDATE AGE OF SUBJECT +D20 ....................................

: 3 UPGRADE AGE BAND TO HIGHER .......................................

: 4 IF [END]
     U P D A T E   C Y C L E   C O M P L E T E D
     ALL PERSONNEL RECORDS SYNCED
    ELSEIF [OK]
     C O N T I N U E   R U N N I N G   U P D A T E ? [Y/N]
    ELSE
     I N V A L I D   S T A T E  -  C O N T A C T   S Y S A D M I N

---------------------------------------------------------------------------
== END OF SYSTEM OUTPUT — DOT-MATRIX HARD COPY (REV 72DPI) ==
---------------------------------------------------------------------------
```

Once the subject data update retrieval is complete, they are ready to participate in memetic simulations.
